
# UyghurDaily Article Structure

All posts live under `blog/` as Markdown files:

- `blog/YYYY-MM-DD-title.md`

Example:
- `blog/2026-02-18-diaspora-voice.md`

Each article uses this front matter and structure:

```markdown
---
title: "بۈگۈنكى ئۇيغۇر ئەھۋالى: [تېما]"
date: YYYY-MM-DD
tags: [uyghur, diaspora, genocide]
lang: ug
---

## كىرىش

2–3 sentences: why this topic matters today.

## بۈگۈنكى واقە ياكى تېما

Describe today’s event, news, or personal observation.

## مەنىسى نېمە؟

What this means for Uyghurs and for the world.

## چاقىرىق ياكى ئويلىنىش

Closing thoughts or a call to action.

## English Summary (optional)

3–5 sentence summary in English for non-Uyghur readers.
